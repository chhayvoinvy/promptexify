# Advanced Financial Analysis and Modeling

Utilize Claude's analytical capabilities for sophisticated financial analysis, investment evaluation, and risk management.

## Financial Statement Analysis

### Ratio Analysis Framework

Analyze financial health through:

1. Liquidity ratios (Current, Quick, Cash)
2. Profitability ratios (ROE, ROA, Gross Margin)
3. Leverage ratios (Debt-to-Equity, Interest Coverage)
4. Efficiency ratios (Asset Turnover, Inventory Turnover)
5. Market ratios (P/E, Price-to-Book, Dividend Yield)

### Trend Analysis

Identify patterns in:

- Revenue growth trajectories
- Profit margin evolution
- Cash flow sustainability
- Working capital management
- Capital allocation efficiency

## Investment Valuation

### DCF Modeling

Build comprehensive models including:

- Free cash flow projections
- Terminal value calculations
- Discount rate determination
- Sensitivity analysis
- Scenario planning

### Comparative Analysis

Evaluate investments through:

- Industry peer comparisons
- Historical performance metrics
- Market multiple analysis
- Risk-adjusted returns
- Beta and correlation studies

## Risk Assessment

### Portfolio Risk Management

- Diversification optimization
- Correlation analysis
- Value at Risk (VaR) calculations
- Stress testing scenarios
- Hedge ratio determination

### Credit Risk Evaluation

- Default probability modeling
- Credit rating analysis
- Collateral valuation
- Recovery rate estimation
- Exposure assessment
