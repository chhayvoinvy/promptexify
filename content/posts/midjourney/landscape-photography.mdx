# Breathtaking Landscape Photography

Master the art of landscape photography with these professional Midjourney prompts for capturing nature's beauty.

## The Perfect Landscape

### Mountain Sunrise

A majestic mountain range at sunrise with golden light illuminating snow-capped peaks, dramatic clouds in the sky, a serene alpine lake in the foreground reflecting the mountains, shot with wide-angle lens, landscape photography style, HDR processing, vivid colors, National Geographic quality --ar 16:9 --v 6

### Seascape Drama

Capture powerful ocean scenes with crashing waves, dramatic storm clouds, and golden hour lighting.
